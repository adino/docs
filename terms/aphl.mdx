## APHL
APHL (Association of Public Health Laboratories) is a national nonprofit organization that represents governmental laboratories working to protect public health. Its members include state, local, and federal labs that test for infectious diseases, monitor environmental threats, and support emergency responses. APHL provides training, standards, and advocacy to strengthen the laboratory network across the United States and globally. By connecting labs, healthcare systems, and policymakers, it ensures faster, more accurate detection of health threats. APHL is critical to safeguarding communities and advancing the future of public health infrastructure.
