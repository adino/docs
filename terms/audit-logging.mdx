## Audit Logging

`Audit Logging` is the process of recording detailed logs of system events, user actions, and changes to ensure accountability, security, and compliance.

Included in Augit Logging is the act of creating an `Audit Trail` to specifically audit how users interact with a system to create, read, update and delete data.
